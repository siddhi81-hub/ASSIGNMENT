Welcome to Thakur College

Thakur College of Engineering and Technology (TCET) is an autonomous, Mumbai-based private engineering college established in 2001, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. programs in engineering, IT, and management

Explore our wide range of *academic programs* on the Courses page, and see our beautiful campus in the Gallery!

Our Academic Programs (Courses)

We offer specialized programs across technology, business, and arts. All courses are taught by experienced faculty.

  • Computer Science (B.Tech/M.Tech)

    Focuses on software engineering, data structures, AI, and machine learning.

  • Business Administration (BBA/MBA)

    Covers finance, marketing, human resources, and strategic management.

  • Ph.D.:

    coursework in research methodology, advanced subject-specific topics, and research ethics

  • Electrical Engineering

    Advanced study in power systems, circuit design, and telecommunications.

  • Data Science

    Intensive training in statistical modeling, big data analytics, and programming.
